# Enterprise AI Branch Onboarding (10 Minutes)

Last updated: April 8, 2026
Audience: Branch managers, shift leads, operations staff
Goal: Use Enterprise AI every day to make faster branch decisions.

## 1. What This Tool Helps You Do
Enterprise AI helps you:
- See which branch actions matter most today.
- Catch high-risk alerts early.
- Get clear next steps from the assistant.
- Turn suggestions into tracked tasks.

## 2. Your Daily 10-Minute Routine
Do this at start of shift.

1. Open Enterprise AI Console.
2. Check alerts in this order:
- P1 first (urgent)
- P2 second (important today)
- P3 last (watch list)
3. Open Branch Performance Insights.
4. Ask one focused assistant question.
5. Create at least 1 action item with owner and due date.

## 3. How to Ask Better Questions
Use simple and specific questions.

Good pattern:
- What area?
- What time period?
- What result do you need?

Examples:
- "Which branch actions can improve margin this week?"
- "What should we restock in the next 7 days to avoid stockouts?"
- "Where is expense risk highest right now and what should we do first?"

Avoid:
- "How is business?"
- "Give me everything"

## 4. Alert Handling Rules
- P1: act now and notify admin.
- P2: assign action today.
- P3: monitor and include in weekly review.

If same P1 alert repeats:
- Escalate immediately to BUSINESS_ADMIN or SUPER_ADMIN.

## 5. Action Tracker Rules
Every important recommendation should become an action.

Required fields:
- Owner
- Due date
- Short note

Status meanings:
- TODO: not started
- IN_PROGRESS: working on it
- BLOCKED: waiting for help/input
- DONE: completed
- CANCELLED: no longer needed

## 6. Weekly Team Check (15 Minutes)
Once per week, review:
- Overdue actions
- Actions completed
- What worked
- What to fix next week

## 7. Fast Troubleshooting
If AI suggestions feel weak:
1. Ask a more specific question.
2. Include a time period (7 days or 30 days).
3. Focus on one branch or one category.

If there are too many alerts:
1. Tell your admin team.
2. Ask them to tune alert policy (priority and dedupe windows).

## 8. Success Target for Branch Teams
By end of month:
- Fewer overdue tasks
- Faster response to P1/P2 alerts
- Better margin or lower avoidable costs
